# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analysis--Excel

An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing 6 months of transaction-level sales data from a multi-location coffee shop chain. Built with PivotTables, PivotCharts, and slicers to explore sales trends, product performance, store footfall, and customer ordering behavior.

Overview
This project turns raw point-of-sale transaction data into a single-page, decision-ready dashboard. It covers 149,116 transactions worth $698,812.33 in total sales across 3 store locations (Astoria, Hell's Kitchen, and Lower Manhattan) from January to June 2023.

Key Insights
Sales are growing month over month — from ~$81.7K in January to ~$166.5K in June, more than doubling over the period.
Peak ordering hours are 9 AM–10 AM, with a steady decline through the afternoon and evening.
Coffee is the top category, making up 39% of sales, followed by Tea (28%) and Bakery (12%).
Barista Espresso is the best-selling product by total bill, ahead of Brewed Chai Tea and Gourmet Brewed Coffee.
Sales and footfall are fairly evenly split across all three store locations, with Hell's Kitchen slightly ahead.
Regular and Large sizes dominate order size distribution (31% and 30% respectively).
